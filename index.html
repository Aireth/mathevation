The System

MATHevation is a web-based application has a purpose of teaching students more about math and further developing their skills through motivation by using gamification and reward systems. Users can obtain learning materials while being able to engage in mathematics-based game activities to boost motivative engagement of users to the world of mathematics in Probability.

About Us

The researchers are students in Mapua Institute of Technology. The study's goal is to develop a gamified web application utilizing Unity and Visual Studio that will deliver more interactive information and widen the topics covered compared to standard learning methods.

System Overview

Designed for Grade 8 students, this web app gamifies foundational probability concepts. Students are motivated by points, difficulty levels, and rewards as they tackle interactive lessons. The app tracks their progress, fostering a more engaging and effective learning experience for probability in Grade 8. Educators can monitor student performance and manage content through an administrator panel, ensuring the app stays relevant and engaging.

System Requirements

Are you ready to unlock the fun of probability with Mathevation? Here's what you'll need:

Device: Laptops, desktops, or even tablets work! A bigger screen (10 inches+) is recommended for a smoother learning experience.

Operating System: Most modern systems are compatible, including Windows 10+, and macOS 12+

Web Browser: Chrome, Firefox, or Safari are all great choices.

Internet Connection: An internet connection is key to accessing all the app's features.

We're constantly improving Mathevation, so these requirements might change in the future. We'll keep you posted!

User Manual

This app helps you learn probability through awesome games and challenges. Let's get started!

Logging In:

Go to www.mathevation.online and create an account. Once you have an account, log in with your username and password.

Exploring the App:

Look around! There are different sections for learning and playing. The dashboard shows your points and completed activities.

Learning Probability:

Each section focuses on a different probability concept. You'll find fun games, and activities to help you learn. Earn points and level up as you complete activities!

Keeping Track:

Your dashboard shows your progress and points. See how much you've learned and how far you've come!
